About Voya

A story of wanderlust turned into a mission.

Our Story

Born from a Passion to Explore

Voya started in 2011 when two friends — Elena and David — returned from an impromptu six-week journey through Southeast Asia with no plan and nothing but a backpack. What they discovered was that travel, done thoughtfully, changes you.

They built Voya because they were tired of seeing travel agencies sell destinations like products. They wanted to create a company that thought about the whole experience — the culture, the people, the quiet moments that don't make it onto Instagram.

Today, Voya has helped over 8,000 travelers discover the world in a way that feels genuine, personal, and unforgettable.
